# Third Party Auditor Engagement

This document defines the process for engaging the SOC 2 auditor.

## Selection

- Evaluate auditor experience with blockchain and cloud infrastructure.
- Confirm independence and acceptable scope coverage.
- Finalize engagement letter and audit timeline.

## Coordination

- Provide system description, control matrix, and evidence manifest.
- Schedule walkthroughs with control owners.
- Align on sampling methodology and evidence format.

## Deliverables

- SOC 2 Type II report with auditor opinion.
- Management letter for improvement items.
