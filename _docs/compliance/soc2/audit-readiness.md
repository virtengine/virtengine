# Audit Readiness Assessment

This assessment confirms that SOC 2 controls are implemented, operating, and supported by evidence.

## Readiness Checklist

- Control objectives documented and approved.
- Control owners identified with review cadence.
- Evidence collection automated and validated.
- Incident response and monitoring processes operational.
- Vendor risk management and access reviews current.

## Readiness Activities

- Walkthrough of control execution for sample period.
- Evidence sampling to validate completeness and quality.
- Remediation tracking for any deficiencies.

## Outputs

- Readiness report with risks and remediation plan.
- Updated control matrix and evidence manifest.
