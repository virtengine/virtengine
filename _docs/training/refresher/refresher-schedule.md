# Quarterly Refresher Training Schedule

**Version:** 1.0.0  
**Date:** 2026-01-31

---

## Overview

Quarterly refresher training ensures operators maintain current knowledge and skills. Each quarter focuses on a different aspect of VirtEngine operations, with mandatory attendance for certification maintenance.

---

## Annual Schedule

| Quarter | Focus Area | Duration | Format |
|---------|------------|----------|--------|
| Q1 (Jan-Mar) | Security Updates & Threats | 3 hours | Hybrid |
| Q2 (Apr-Jun) | Performance & Optimization | 3 hours | Hybrid |
| Q3 (Jul-Sep) | Incident Response Drills | 4 hours | In-person/Remote Live |
| Q4 (Oct-Dec) | New Features & Upgrades | 3 hours | Hybrid |

## Q3: Incident Response Drills

**Schedule:** Third week of August  
**Duration:** 4 hours  
**Format:** Live simulation exercises

### Agenda

| Time | Topic | Format |
|------|-------|--------|
| 0:00-0:30 | Incident response review | Presentation |
| 0:30-0:45 | Drill briefing | Presentation |
| 0:45-1:45 | Drill 1: SEV-2 Scenario | Simulation |
| 1:45-2:00 | Break | - |
| 2:00-2:30 | Drill 1 debrief | Discussion |
| 2:30-3:30 | Drill 2: SEV-1 Scenario | Simulation |
| 3:30-4:00 | Drill 2 debrief & wrap-up | Discussion |

### Drill Scenarios

**Drill 1 (SEV-2):** Degraded service performance
- Simulated high latency
- Partial feature unavailability
- Practice detection, diagnosis, mitigation

**Drill 2 (SEV-1):** Major outage or security incident
- Simulated service outage OR
- Simulated security breach
- Full incident response exercise

### Roles Practiced

| Role | Requirements |
|------|--------------|
| Incident Commander | CVO or CPO certified |
| Technical Lead | All operators |
| Communications | All operators |
| Scribe | All operators |

### Assessment

- Participation in both drills
- Role-appropriate performance
- Debrief contribution
- Pass/Fail based on participation

## Registration & Attendance

### Registration

1. Register at training.virtengine.com
2. Select preferred session date/time
3. Confirm attendance 1 week before
4. Receive calendar invite and materials

### Attendance Policy

- **Required** for certification maintenance
- Makeup sessions available within 30 days
- Three consecutive misses require re-certification
- Remote attendance option for all sessions

### Make-up Sessions

If unable to attend scheduled session:
1. Request make-up within 30 days
2. Complete self-paced materials
3. Schedule assessment with proctor
4. Pass assessment
