## Accessing Videos

### Internal Access

1. Navigate to training.virtengine.com
2. Log in with your operator credentials
3. Select "Video Library" from menu
4. Search or browse by category

### Offline Access

For offline viewing:
1. Request download token from training@virtengine.com
2. Use VirtEngine Training App
3. Download videos for offline playback
4. Content expires after 30 days

### Playback Features

- Variable speed (0.5x - 2x)
- Closed captions (English)
- Chapter navigation
- Bookmarking
- Notes and annotations
- Progress tracking

## Video Production

### Contributing Content

Operators can contribute training content:
1. Submit proposal to training@virtengine.com
2. Content review by training team
3. Recording coordination
4. Post-production and approval
5. Publication and CE credit

### Video Standards

| Aspect | Requirement |
|--------|-------------|
| Resolution | 1080p minimum |
| Audio | Clear, no background noise |
| Duration | 20-60 minutes |
| Format | MP4 with chapters |
| Captions | Required |
