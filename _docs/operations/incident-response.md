# Incident Response

This document defines the incident response lifecycle for VirtEngine systems and supporting services.

## Objectives

- Detect and contain security and availability incidents quickly.
- Preserve evidence and ensure accurate communications.
- Restore services safely and prevent recurrence.

## Roles

- Incident Commander: Coordinates response and decisions.
- Scribe: Maintains timeline, artifacts, and action items.
- Comms Lead: Manages internal and external communications.
- Engineering Leads: Execute remediation and recovery tasks.

## Lifecycle

1. Detect and Triage
   - Validate alert, classify severity, and open an incident ticket.
2. Contain and Investigate
   - Limit blast radius and collect logs, metrics, and forensic artifacts.
3. Eradicate and Recover
   - Apply fixes, validate recovery, and monitor for regression.
4. Post Incident Review
   - Document root cause, contributing factors, and corrective actions.

## Evidence

- Incident ticket with severity, timestamps, and owners.
- Timeline with key actions and decisions.
- Post incident review with corrective actions and follow ups.
