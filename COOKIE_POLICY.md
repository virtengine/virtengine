## 2. What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files stored on your device (computer, smartphone, tablet) when you visit websites. Cookies help websites remember your preferences, improve functionality, and analyze usage patterns.

**Types of Cookies by Duration:**
- **Session Cookies:** Temporary cookies deleted when you close your browser
- **Persistent Cookies:** Remain on your device for a set period or until manually deleted

**Types of Cookies by Purpose:**
- **First-Party Cookies:** Set by VirtEngine
- **Third-Party Cookies:** Set by external services we use

## 3. How We Use Cookies

### 3.1 Essential Cookies (Strictly Necessary)

**Purpose:** Required for core service functionality  
**Consent Required:** No (technically necessary for service delivery)  
**Duration:** Session or up to 1 year

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `session_id` | Maintain user session state | Session |
| `auth_token` | Authenticate API requests | 24 hours |
| `csrf_token` | Prevent cross-site request forgery | Session |
| `wallet_connection` | Remember wallet connection preference | 7 days |
| `consent_preferences` | Store cookie consent choices | 1 year |

**Blockchain Wallet Cookies:**
- `keplr_connection`: Remember Keplr wallet connection
- `ledger_connection`: Remember Ledger hardware wallet connection

### 3.2 Analytics and Performance Cookies

**Purpose:** Understand how users interact with the Services to improve performance  
**Consent Required:** Yes (opt-in or opt-out depending on jurisdiction)  
**Duration:** Up to 26 months

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Provider | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|----------|
| `_ga` | Google Analytics - distinguish users | Google | 2 years |
| `_gid` | Google Analytics - distinguish users | Google | 24 hours |
| `_gat` | Google Analytics - throttle request rate | Google | 1 minute |
| `analytics_id` | Internal analytics tracking | VirtEngine | 26 months |
| `usage_metrics` | Track feature usage patterns | VirtEngine | 26 months |

**What We Analyze:**
- Pages visited and time spent
- Navigation paths and user flows
- Feature usage and interaction patterns
- Error rates and performance metrics
- Geographic distribution (country-level, not precise location)
- Device and browser types

**Privacy-Focused Analytics:**
We prioritize privacy-focused analytics solutions that:
- Do not track users across websites
- Anonymize IP addresses
- Do not create persistent user profiles
- Respect Do Not Track (DNT) signals

### 3.3 Functionality Cookies

**Purpose:** Remember your preferences and settings  
**Consent Required:** Yes (implied consent for preference storage)  
**Duration:** Up to 1 year

| Cookie Name | Purpose | Duration |
|-------------|---------|----------|
| `language_pref` | Remember language selection | 1 year |
| `theme_mode` | Remember dark/light mode preference | 1 year |
| `network_selection` | Remember mainnet/testnet preference | 30 days |
| `notification_settings` | Store notification preferences | 1 year |
| `dashboard_layout` | Remember dashboard customization | 6 months |

### 3.4 Marketing and Advertising Cookies

**Purpose:** Deliver relevant advertisements and measure campaign effectiveness  
**Consent Required:** Yes (explicit opt-in in most jurisdictions)  
**Current Status:** **We do not currently use marketing/advertising cookies**

If we implement marketing cookies in the future, we will:
- Obtain explicit consent before placement
- Update this policy with details
- Provide clear opt-out mechanisms
- Honor Do Not Sell (CCPA) and objection rights (GDPR)

## 4. Similar Technologies

### 4.1 Local Storage and Session Storage

We use HTML5 local storage and session storage for:
- Caching wallet addresses and public keys
- Storing UI state (expanded panels, selected tabs)
- Temporarily storing form data to prevent loss
- Caching blockchain data to reduce network requests

**Persistence:**
- Session storage: Cleared when tab/window closes
- Local storage: Persistent until manually cleared

### 4.2 Web Beacons (Pixel Tags)

Small transparent images embedded in emails or web pages to:
- Track email open rates (support communications)
- Measure campaign effectiveness
- Detect fraudulent activity

**Privacy:** We do not use web beacons to track users across websites.

### 4.3 Browser Fingerprinting

**Limited Use:** We collect basic device information for:
- Fraud detection during VEID verification
- Security monitoring (detecting unusual access patterns)
- Browser compatibility testing

**We Do NOT:**
- Create persistent user profiles across sessions
- Track users across websites
- Use fingerprinting for advertising

## 6. Managing Cookie Preferences

### 6.1 Cookie Consent Tool

**First Visit:** Upon your first visit, you'll see a cookie consent banner allowing you to:
- Accept all cookies
- Accept only essential cookies
- Customize cookie preferences by category
- View this Cookie Policy

**Manage Preferences:** Change your cookie settings at any time:
- Click "Cookie Settings" in the website footer
- Access "Privacy Settings" in your account dashboard
- Email privacy@virtengine.com for assistance

### 6.2 Browser Controls

**Disable Cookies:** Configure your browser to block or delete cookies:
- **Chrome:** Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies
- **Firefox:** Preferences > Privacy & Security > Cookies
- **Safari:** Preferences > Privacy > Cookies
- **Edge:** Settings > Privacy > Cookies

**Important:** Disabling essential cookies may prevent core functionality (authentication, transactions).

**Do Not Track (DNT):** We respect Do Not Track signals for non-essential cookies.

### 6.3 Mobile Device Settings

**iOS:** Settings > Safari > Privacy & Security > Block All Cookies  
**Android:** Chrome App > Settings > Site Settings > Cookies

### 6.4 Opt-Out Links

**Google Analytics Opt-Out:**  
Install the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on:  
https://tools.google.com/dlpage/gaoptout

## 7. Cookie Retention and Deletion
